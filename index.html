Manual - Discover the universe

About game

Discover the universe is a simple web game. The aim of this game is to get the rocket into space and launch the first satellite. The game is based on a HTML Canvas and JavaScript.

Danger

Flight through cloud can damage the cover of rocket. Storm clouds damaging especially electronics. Conflict with meteorites greatly harms the rocket. It is necessary to keep the racket in the field of view, otherwise you lose the connection with the racket.

Control

The rocket is controlled by arrows. All other information is displayed in the info panel. Here you can find an indication of damage, speed, height and console.
